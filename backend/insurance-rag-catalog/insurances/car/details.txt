Car Insurance
Coverage Levels:

- Basic Coverage: Covers third-party liability, some collision, and theft. Essential protection for others and basic support in accidents. insurance costs 50 dollar per month
- Advanced Coverage: Adds comprehensive protection for the vehicle, including more extensive collision and roadside assistance. insurance costs 70 dollar per month
- Top Coverage: Includes complete collision, theft, comprehensive support, personal injury protection, and extended roadside services. insurance costs 100 dollar per month
